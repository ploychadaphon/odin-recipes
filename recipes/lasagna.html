Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until browned; drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce with the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with the egg and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, then layer noodles, ricotta mix, and mozzarella; repeat layers.
  6. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  7. Uncover and bake another 10 minutes, then let rest before serving.
