Tom Yum

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, chopped
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves, torn
  • 1-inch galangal, sliced
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 Thai chilies, crushed
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add lemongrass, lime leaves, and galangal; simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms and chilies; cook for 3 more minutes.
  4. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in fish sauce and lime juice to taste.
  6. Remove from heat and discard lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves if desired.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
