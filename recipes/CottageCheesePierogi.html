Cottage Cheese Pirogi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 egg yolk (for filling)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter and onions for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl; add egg, water, and oil to form a soft dough.
  2. Knead dough until smooth; cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Mix cottage cheese with egg yolk, salt, and pepper to make the filling.
  4. Roll out dough thinly and cut into circles (about 3 inches wide).
  5. Place a spoonful of filling in the center, fold over, and seal edges well.
  6. Boil pierogi in salted water until they float, about 3–5 minutes.
  7. Serve with melted butter and sautéed onions, if desired.
